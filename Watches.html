Collection: Tambour

Style:The Louis Vuitton Tambour Convergence has an elegant, vintage-inspired style with a contemporary flair, drawing on classic montres à guichet (window watches) design while maintaining modern proportions and finishing. It is considered an elegant dress watch that is comfortable and easy to wear daily.

Case:It uses the signature drum-shaped Tambour case, but in a slimmer, more refined 37mm diameter and 8mm thickness. The lugs are a distinct feature, with polished outer surfaces and frosted, sandblasted inner concave curves, inspired by vintage "flame" or "claw" lugs.

Design Options: The Louis Vuitton Tambour Convergence collection is available in three distinct design variations.
